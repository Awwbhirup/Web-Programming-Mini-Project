Emergency & First Aid

Immediate actions to take in case of an accident.

What To Do?

Turn on hazard lights. If possible, move vehicles to the side of the road. Place warning triangles at least 45 meters behind the vehicle to warn oncoming traffic.

Check yourself and passengers for injuries. Do not move anyone who is unconscious or has neck pain unless there is an immediate danger like fire.

Call 112 or local emergency numbers immediately. Provide location, number of injured, and nature of injuries clearly.

Exchange names, contact details, and insurance information with other drivers involved. Take photos of the damage and the scene for documentation.

If you see smoke or fire, evacuate everyone to a safe distance (at least 30 meters). Do not attempt to extinguish a large fire yourself.

Keep injured persons warm and calm. Do not give them food or water. Talk to them reassuringly until help arrives.
