Student Registration
1. Full Name

1Centered Card Layout

The entire page isolates the user's focus on a single central card element, minimizing distractions to improve conversation rates.

2Data Collection

Standard form inputs collect user identity. "Age" has validation (min/max), and "License Type" allows selection between Learner and Permanent.

3Action Button

A prominent "Start Learning" button submits the data and acts as the gateway to the course content.
